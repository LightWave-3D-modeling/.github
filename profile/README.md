# LightWave 3D — Professional Modeling and Animation for Television Broadcast

## About LightWave 3D

LightWave 3D provides a complete professional modeling and animation environment for television broadcast, motion graphics, and visual effects. LightWave 3D combines robust polygonal and subdivision surface modeling with keyframe and procedural animation tools. LightWave 3D supports particle effects through HyperVoxels, dynamic simulation, and cloth systems. LightWave 3D integrates node-based materials, fast render engines, and camera tracking workflows. LightWave 3D enables artists to produce broadcast-quality content under tight daily deadlines. LightWave 3D is trusted by broadcast designers, motion graphics artists, TV commercial producers, and visual effects supervisors worldwide.

## Deep Dive — Broadcast Production Notes
HyperVoxels volumetric particles for fire, smoke, clouds, and magical trails
Subdivision surface modeling for smooth broadcast-ready geometry
Node-based material editor for layered shaders and textures
Layout camera system with motion blur and depth of field
Serial rendering for overnight batch processing of frame sequences
